About us

Founded by Ava Green and a group of close friends who shared a passion for clean eating and sustainable living, GreenFork Café was created to make healthy, eco-friendly food both accessible and enjoyable. What began as Ava’s dream to bring farm-fresh, wholesome meals to her community has grown into a welcoming space for young professionals, health-conscious families, and environmentally mindful eaters.

Our mission is simple: serve delicious, locally sourced dishes that nourish people and protect the planet. At GreenFork Café, every bite supports a healthier you—and a greener world.


About Our Ingredients

At GreenFork Café, we believe that food should be as good for the planet as it is for you. We’re a trendy vegan café serving vibrant plant-based meals, refreshing smoothies, and indulgent desserts—all made with locally sourced, sustainable ingredients. Our produce comes straight from nearby farms, ensuring every bite is fresh, flavorful, and full of nutrition. We partner with eco-conscious suppliers, use compostable packaging, and make it our mission to minimize waste in every step of our process. Beyond great taste, we’re passionate about community and creativity—crafting dishes that not only nourish your body but also look beautiful enough to share. At GreenFork Café, sustainability meets style, one colorful plate at a time.
